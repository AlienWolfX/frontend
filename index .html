Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I rent a car from your company?

    Renting a car with us is easy! You can either book online through our user-friendly website or visit one of our conveniently located branches. Follow the simple steps, and you'll be on the road in no time.

  • Can I choose the specific car model I want to rent?

    Absolutely! Our online booking system allows you to select the car model that suits your preferences and needs. We offer a diverse range of vehicles to cater to different tastes and requirements.

  • What is included in the rental price?

    Our rental prices typically include basic insurance coverage and 24/7 roadside assistance. Additional options, such as GPS navigation and child seats, are available for a nominal fee. You can review the inclusions and customize your rental accordingly.

  • How do I return the rental car?

    Returning the car is simple. Just bring it back to the designated drop-off location at the agreed-upon time. If you need flexibility, we offer options for extended rental periods, and our staff will assist you with the return process.

  • Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

    Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation online or by contacting our customer service. We understand that plans can change, and we strive to provide flexibility to accommodate your needs.
